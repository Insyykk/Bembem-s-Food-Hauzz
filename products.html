History of Bembem's Food Hauz'

Bembem’s Food Hauz was founded on August 3, 2017 in Boljoon, Cebu by Maria Theresa Atabay Alemania. The business started as a small food establishment with the goal of providing affordable, delicious, and home-cooked meals to the local community. With limited resources at the beginning, the owner relied on hard work, dedication, and a passion for cooking to build the restaurant. As more customers began to appreciate the taste and quality of the food, Bembem’s Food Hauz gradually gained popularity. Through word of mouth and loyal customers, the business grew and expanded its menu to serve a wider variety of dishes. Despite facing challenges such as competition and daily operational struggles, the restaurant continued to improve its services and maintain customer satisfaction. Today, Bembem’s Food Hauz is known as a trusted local restaurant in Boljoon, Cebu, reflecting perseverance, determination, and the owner’s commitment to serving the community.

How did the government of the Philippines help your business grow?

The Government of the Philippines helped the growth of Bembem’s Food Hauz by providing support programs for small businesses. Through local government units (LGUs), the business benefited from business registration assistance, permits, and guidance on legal operations. Government agencies also offer livelihood programs, trainings, and seminars that help improve food safety, management, and entrepreneurship skills. In addition, access to small business loans and financial assistance helped support daily operations and business improvement. These government efforts helped the business become more stable and competitive in the community.
